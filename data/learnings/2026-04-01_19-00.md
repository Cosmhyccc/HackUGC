## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** consistently dominates at 14-21% engagement across all formats — screen time reclamation, family safety, and fitness breakthroughs outperform entertainment by 2-3x regardless of view count, making health the highest-conversion category for app UGC.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieves 23-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable humor or cute moments (PNW roadtrip 28.33%, iPhone moments 23.89%, cute animals 23.42%) — never use for feature tutorials or direct app promotion.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** beats pure feature demos at 17-20% engagement when leading with transformation or comedic relief rather than features (Spotify organizer 17.98%, personal transformation 18.11%, fitness transformation 15.68%) — outperforms direct tutorials by 4-5x.
4. **Direct Feature Tutorials** (wallpaper downloads, settings navigation, step-by-step CTAs) consistently collapse to 3-7% engagement — users actively reject instruction-heavy content; prioritize emotional transformation narratives and problem-solving instead.
5. **Hidden Feature Reveals** (privacy settings, screen time controls, insider functionality) achieve 17-21% engagement (privacy reveals 14.99%, family safety 16.07%) and signal genuine problem-solving — position UGC around solutions users actively seek, not aesthetic customization.
6. **Talking Head + Insider Knowledge** drives 18-22% engagement when delivering vulnerability, exclusive problem-solving, or relatable rants (religious/cultural 19.52%, privacy 14.99%, family safety 16.07%) — beats instruction-heavy content by 2.5x.
7. **Hook + Demo Hybrid** with visual transformation beats pure feature demos at 12-16% engagement — fitness before/after and food creation hooks generate 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity alone; pair emotional hooks with visible app results.
8. **Small View Count + Health Category** significantly outperforms large Entertainment videos for app conversion — a 1M-view health video at 20% engagement converts better than a 14M-view entertainment video at 5% engagement; prioritize conversion intent over viral reach.
9. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach (8M+ views) but collapses to 4-8% engagement with zero app conversion — avoid entirely for app-specific UGC regardless of initial view count; this format kills conversion intent despite high visibility.
10. **Relatable Humor + Silent Format** in Lifestyle reaches 23-28% engagement with zero voiceover required (cute animals 23.42%, PNW travel 28.33%) — this format compounds emotional connection with accessibility, making it ideal for wellness and screen time apps.
11. **Educational + Emotional Frustration Hook** underperforms at 2-10% engagement in most learning contexts — the exception is relatable rants about difficulty (Spanish learning frustration 15.58%) which suggest emotional authenticity matters infinitely more than instructional clarity for education apps.
12. **Comparison Hooks** (app A vs app B, quality reveals) achieve 9-12% engagement and build credibility (Alight Motion vs CapCut 12.44%) — use when positioning against known competitors to signal genuine problem-solving without direct instruction.
