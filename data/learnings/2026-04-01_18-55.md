# HackUGC Learnings — Wednesday, April 1, 2026
*80 videos classified*


## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** dominates engagement at 14-21% across all formats — screen time reclamation, family safety, and fitness breakthroughs outperform entertainment by 2-3x regardless of view count.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** hits 23-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable humor or cute moments (PNW roadtrip 28.33%, iPhone moments 23.89%) — never use for feature tutorials or direct app promotion.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** achieves 17-20% engagement when leading with emotional or comedic relief rather than features (Spotify organizer 17.98%, alarm comedian 12.04%) — outperforms wallpaper tutorials by 4-5x.
4. **Talking Head + Insider Knowledge** drives 18-22% engagement when delivering vulnerability or exclusive problem-solving (privacy reveals 14.99%, family safety 16.07%, religious/cultural 19.52%) — beats instruction-heavy content by 2.5x.
5. **Hook + Demo Hybrid** with visual transformation beats pure feature demos at 12-16% engagement — fitness before/after and food creation hooks generate 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity alone.
6. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach (8M+ views) but collapses to 4-8% engagement with zero app conversion — avoid entirely for app-specific UGC regardless of initial view count.
7. **Direct Feature Tutorials** (wallpaper downloads, settings navigation, step-by-step CTAs) consistently underperform at 3-7% engagement — users reject instruction-heavy content; prioritize transformation narratives instead.
8. **Hidden Feature Reveals** (privacy settings, screen time controls, insider functionality) achieve 17-21% engagement and signal genuine problem-solving — position UGC around solutions users actively seek, not aesthetic customization.
9. **Comparison Hooks** (app A vs app B, quality reveals) achieve 9-12% engagement and build credibility — use when positioning against known competitors like CapCut to provide reassurance without direct instruction.
10. **Relatable Humor + Silent Format** in Lifestyle reaches 23-28% engagement with zero voiceover required — this format compounds emotional connection with accessibility, making it ideal for apps addressing screen time or wellness pain points.
11. **Small View Count + Health Category** significantly outperforms Entertainment across all formats — a 1M-view health video at 20% engagement converts better than a 14M-view entertainment video at 5% engagement; prioritize conversion intent over viral reach.
12. **Educational + Emotional Frustration Hook** underperforms at 2-10% engagement in most contexts — the exception is relatable rants about difficulty (Spanish learning 15.58%) which suggest emotional authenticity matters more than instructional clarity for learning apps.
