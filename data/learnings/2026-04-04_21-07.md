## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** dominates at 25-35% engagement when apps solve niche identity problems (sobriety 35.18%, faith 25.24%) — position as life-changing for specific communities, never as feature collections.
2. **Hook+Demo Hybrids** outperform pure feature demos by 3-4x (17-35% vs 3-10%) — always pair emotional hooks or problem statements with visible app results; never showcase features without narrative framing.
3. **Talking Head + Authentic Vulnerability** drives 15-18% engagement when creators share relatable frustration or unintended use cases (dad jokes 18.51%, positioning for unexpected benefit 10.53%) — authenticity and emotional vulnerability outperform instructional clarity by 2.5x.
4. **Entertainment apps collapse to 0.41-1.07% engagement with direct CTAs** regardless of view count (1.2M views = 0.41% eng) — hide the app reveal until the final frame and lead with absurdist hooks or relatable commentary instead.
5. **Direct Feature Tutorials consistently collapse to 2-10% engagement** across all categories (language 3.83-5.61%, app demos 3.28%, iPad games 2.93%) — this format is actively rejected; reframe every tutorial as problem-solving or transformation, not instruction.
6. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics achieve 14-21% engagement exclusively when tied to relatable moments** (bear care 20.70%, aesthetic discovery 18.14%, Buzz walkie-talkie 14.73%) — this format is niche-critical but fragile; collapse to 1-3% for feature tips or non-narrative content.
7. **Slideshow + Curated Lists achieve 10-15% engagement as the safest middle ground** (trending apps 14.80%, daily-use apps 11.80%, top 5 picks 12.99%) — this format outperforms pure demos (3-5%) without the niche dependency of Hook+Demo, making it ideal for risk-averse teams.
8. **B2B and Finance apps underperform across all formats (0.12-10.98%)** except when positioned as accessible/creative solutions (AI mastery 10.98%, side hustle 8.95%) — niche positioning and democratization messaging significantly outperform direct CTAs and feature clarity.
9. **Silent UGC + Niche Community Signaling through hashtags drives 11-20% engagement** (recovery app 9.86%, curiosity hashtag 18.14%, CapCut hashtag showcase 12.36%) — strategic hashtag pairing is essential for reaching underserved audiences in lifestyle and health categories.
10. **Comparison + Credibility Positioning achieves 11% engagement while building trust** (competitive positioning 11.14%, partnership social proof 7.43%) — position solutions and competitive advantages over feature clarity or viral trends.
11. **Behind-the-scenes brand takeovers and exclusive deal framing collapse to 3.92-7.32% engagement** despite massive reach (2.3M views = 3.92%) — these formats feel inauthentic; instead, lead with creator-built positioning or community-specific value.
12. **Viral text-heavy content (news, trends, political) generates 1M-14M views but collapses to 1-3% engagement with zero conversion intent** (trending apps 14.80% views but likely low conversion) — avoid entirely; these drive visibility, not customers.
