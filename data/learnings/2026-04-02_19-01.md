## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** consistently drives 15-20% engagement across all formats and dominates conversion intent — prioritize screen time reclamation, family safety, and fitness breakthroughs over entertainment regardless of view count.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieves 20-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable or cute moments (PNW roadtrips 28.33%, cute animals 23.42%) — never use for feature tutorials or direct app promotion.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** beats pure feature demos at 17-20% engagement by leading with transformation or comedic relief — outperforms direct tutorials by 4-5x, making narrative framing essential for demo content.
4. **Direct Feature Tutorials** consistently collapse to 3-9% engagement across all categories including new data — users actively reject instruction-heavy content; prioritize emotional narratives or problem-solving positioning instead.
5. **Talking Head + Relatable Rant** drives 15-19% engagement when delivering vulnerability or authentic frustration (Spanish learning rant 15.58%, dad jokes 18.51%) — emotional authenticity outperforms instructional clarity by 2.5x.
6. **Comparison Hooks** (app vs competitor positioning) achieve 9-12% engagement and build credibility without direct instruction — use to signal genuine problem-solving over feature clarity.
7. **Hook + Demo Hybrid** with visual transformation beats pure feature demos at 12-20% engagement — pair emotional hooks with visible app results to drive 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity alone.
8. **Small view count Health videos** significantly outperform large Entertainment videos for app conversion — a 1M-view health video at 18%+ engagement converts better than a 10M+ view entertainment video at 5-7% engagement.
9. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach but collapses to 1-3% engagement with zero conversion intent — avoid entirely for app UGC; this format kills conversion despite high visibility.
10. **Animation + Absurdist Humor** achieves 25% engagement for entertainment (circus concept 25.00%) — explore surreal, unexpected creative approaches for entertainment apps, but validate conversion intent separately from engagement metrics.
11. **Silent UGC + Niche Community Signaling** through hashtags drives 12-20% engagement when targeting specific audiences (recovery app 9.86%, wellness 14%) — use aesthetic content paired with strategic hashtags to reach underserved communities.
12. **Rare feature reveals + problem-solving positioning** achieves 10-20% engagement when highlighting solutions users actively seek (hidden features 10.56%, app positioning 16.18%) — signal genuine value over aesthetic customization.
