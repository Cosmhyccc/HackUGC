## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** consistently dominates conversion intent across all formats (10-20% engagement), with small-view health videos (99K-423K views) outperforming massive entertainment videos by 3-5x conversion potential regardless of reach.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieve 20-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable moments (PNW 28.33%, bear care 20.70%, cute animals 17-20%), but collapse to 1-3% when used for feature tutorials or generic product discovery.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** beats pure feature demos at 17-20% engagement (faith app 25.24%, indie Goodreads 18.35%, soccer skills 17.01%), outperforming direct tutorials by 4-5x — narrative framing and problem-solving positioning are non-negotiable.
4. **Direct Feature Tutorials** consistently collapse to 3-9% engagement across all categories (walkie-talkie 4.55%, German fundamentals 9.37%, app features 3.83-5.61%), confirming users actively reject instruction-heavy content regardless of view count or production quality.
5. **Talking Head + Relatable Rant** drives 15-19% engagement when delivering authentic vulnerability or frustration (Spanish rant 15.58%, dad jokes 18.51%, sports commentary 15.05%), with emotional authenticity outperforming instructional clarity by 2.5x.
6. **Animation + Absurdist Humor** achieves 25% engagement for entertainment apps (circus 25.00%), but engagement metrics alone don't predict conversion intent — validate separately and pair with problem-solving positioning for app UGC.
7. **Hook + Demo Hybrid** with visual transformation beats pure feature demos at 12-20% engagement when pairing emotional hooks with visible app results (faith app 25.24%, organizational 16.18%, wellness 14.58%), driving 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity.
8. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach (1M-14M views) but collapses to 1-3% engagement with zero conversion intent (military news 2.31%, political tribute 1.26-1.91%), making it toxic for app UGC despite high visibility.
9. **Comparison Hooks** (app vs competitor positioning) achieve 9-12% engagement while building credibility without direct instruction (language app comparison 7.60%, early access flex 11.14%), signaling genuine problem-solving over feature clarity.
10. **Rare feature reveals + problem-solving positioning** drives 10-20% engagement when highlighting solutions users actively seek (hidden features 10.56%, organizational productivity 10.56%, app-based guides 11.56%), outperforming aesthetic customization by 3x.
11. **Silent UGC + Niche Community Signaling** through hashtags drives 12-20% engagement when targeting underserved audiences (recovery app 9.86%, wellness hashtag 14%, curiosity hashtag 18.14%), making strategic hashtag pairing essential for lifestyle content.
12. **Entertainment apps reject direct promotion** — even with 1M+ views, direct CTAs and feature showcases collapse to 0.41-1.07% engagement; instead, lead with absurdist humor (25%), relatable commentary (15-18%), or cute moments (11-20%) before revealing the app.
