## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** dominates conversion intent across all formats with 10-35% engagement (sobriety app 35.18%, faith app 25.24%, motivational health 10.25%), proving niche community positioning outperforms mass-market entertainment by 3-5x regardless of view count.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieve 14-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable moments (bear care 20.70%, cute animals 20.64%, aesthetic discovery 18.14%), but collapse to 1-3% when used for feature tutorials or generic product showcases.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** beats pure feature demos at 17-25% engagement (faith app 25.24%, indie Goodreads 18.35%, soccer skills 17.01%), outperforming direct tutorials by 4-5x — narrative framing and problem-solving positioning are non-negotiable.
4. **Direct Feature Tutorials** consistently collapse to 2-10% engagement across all categories (German fundamentals 9.37%, language app features 3.83-5.61%, app demo games 3.28%), confirming users actively reject instruction-heavy content regardless of view count or production quality.
5. **Talking Head + Relatable Rant** drives 15-18% engagement when delivering authentic vulnerability or frustration (dad jokes 18.51%, sports commentary 15.05%, language learning struggle 15.58%), with emotional authenticity outperforming instructional clarity by 2.5x.
6. **Entertainment apps reject direct promotion** — even with 1M+ views, direct CTAs and feature showcases collapse to 0.41-1.07% engagement (social app CTA 0.41%, anime apps 1.07%, shopping app 2.01%); instead, lead with absurdist humor (25%), relatable commentary (15-18%), or cute moments (11-20%) before revealing the app.
7. **Comparison Hooks** (app vs competitor positioning) achieve 11% engagement while building credibility without direct instruction (language app comparison 7.60%, early access flex 11.14%), signaling genuine problem-solving over feature clarity — position solutions, not features.
8. **Hook+Demo Hybrids** with visible transformation beat pure feature demos at 12-35% engagement when pairing emotional hooks with app results (sobriety method 35.18%, faith app 25.24%, environmental solution 14.58%), driving 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity alone.
9. **Silent UGC + Niche Community Signaling** through hashtags drives 12-20% engagement when targeting underserved audiences (recovery app 9.86%, wellness hashtag 14%, curiosity hashtag 18.14%, daily apps 11.80%), making strategic hashtag pairing essential for lifestyle content.
10. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach (1M-14M views) but collapses to 1-3% engagement with zero conversion intent (political tribute 1.91%, military news 2.31%, political protest 1.36%), making it toxic for app UGC despite high visibility — avoid news hooks entirely.
11. **Rare feature reveals + problem-solving positioning** drives 10-20% engagement when highlighting solutions users actively seek (organizational productivity 10.56%, app guides 11.56%, hidden features 10.56%), outperforming aesthetic customization by 3x — position apps as solutions, not features.
12. **Slideshow format + curated app lists** achieve 10-15% engagement (trending apps 14.80%, daily-use apps 11.80%, top 4 recommendations 11.80%), making them the safest middle ground between pure demo collapse (3-5%) and Hook+Demo success (17-35%) for risk-averse app teams.
