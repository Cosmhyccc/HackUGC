## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** dominates at 14-21% engagement across all formats — screen time reclamation, family safety, and fitness breakthroughs convert 2-3x better than entertainment regardless of view count, making health the highest-conversion category for app UGC.
2. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieves 23-28% engagement exclusively when paired with relatable humor or cute moments (PNW roadtrip 28.33%, iPhone moments 23.89%, cute animals 23.42%) — never use for feature tutorials or direct app promotion.
3. **App Demo + Emotional Hook** beats pure feature demos at 17-20% engagement when leading with transformation or comedic relief (Spotify organizer 17.98%, personal transformation 18.11%, fitness transformation 15.68%) — outperforms direct tutorials by 4-5x.
4. **Direct Feature Tutorials** consistently collapse to 3-7% engagement across all new data (wallpaper downloads 3.39-4.99%, settings navigation 4.27-8.09%) — users actively reject instruction-heavy content; prioritize emotional transformation narratives instead.
5. **Hidden Feature Reveals + Problem-Solving** achieve 17-21% engagement when positioning around solutions users actively seek (privacy reveals 14.99%, family safety 16.07%) — signal genuine problem-solving, not aesthetic customization.
6. **Talking Head + Insider Knowledge** drives 18-22% engagement when delivering vulnerability, exclusive problem-solving, or relatable rants (religious/cultural 19.52%, privacy 14.99%, family safety 16.07%) — beats instruction-heavy content by 2.5x.
7. **Small View Count + Health Category** significantly outperforms large Entertainment videos for app conversion — a 4.2M-view health video at 20.75% engagement converts better than an 18M-view entertainment video at 5.15% engagement; prioritize conversion intent over viral reach.
8. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach but collapses to 1-7% engagement with zero app conversion intent — avoid entirely for app-specific UGC; this format kills conversion despite high visibility.
9. **Educational + Emotional Frustration Hook** underperforms at 2-10% engagement except for relatable rants (Spanish learning rant 15.58% vs tutorial 3.83%) — emotional authenticity matters infinitely more than instructional clarity for education apps.
10. **Comparison Hooks** (app A vs app B, quality reveals) achieve 9-12% engagement and build credibility (Alight Motion vs CapCut 12.44%) — use when positioning against known competitors to signal genuine problem-solving without direct instruction.
11. **Hook + Demo Hybrid** with visual transformation beats pure feature demos at 12-16% engagement — fitness before/after and food creation hooks generate 2x higher conversion intent than feature clarity alone; pair emotional hooks with visible app results.
12. **Animation + Absurdist Humor** achieves 25% engagement for entertainment (circus concept 25.00%) — explore surreal, unexpected creative approaches for entertainment apps, but validate conversion intent separately from engagement metrics.
