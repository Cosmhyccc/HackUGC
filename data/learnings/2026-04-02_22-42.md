## What We Know

1. **Health + Emotional Transformation** dominates with 25-35% engagement (sobriety 35.18%, faith 25.24%) — position apps as life-changing solutions for niche communities, not feature collections.
2. **Hook+Demo Hybrids** outperform pure feature demos by 3-4x (17-35% vs 3-10% engagement) — always pair emotional hooks or problem statements with visible app results, never showcase features without narrative framing.
3. **Silent UGC + Lifestyle aesthetics** achieve 14-21% engagement exclusively when tied to relatable moments (bear care 20.70%, aesthetic discovery 18.14%) — collapse to 1-3% for feature tutorials, making this format niche-critical but fragile.
4. **Entertainment apps require absurdist hooks or relatable commentary** (15-25% engagement) and must hide the app reveal until the end — direct CTAs and feature showcases collapse to 0.41-1.07% regardless of view count.
5. **Talking Head + Authentic Vulnerability** drives 15-18% engagement (dad jokes 18.51%, language struggles 15.58%) — users prioritize emotional authenticity and relatable frustration over instructional clarity by 2.5x.
6. **Direct Feature Tutorials** consistently collapse to 2-10% engagement across all categories (language app 3.83-5.61%, app demos 3.28%) — this format is actively rejected by audiences; reframe as problem-solving, not instruction.
7. **Slideshow + Curated Lists** achieve 10-15% engagement (trending apps 14.80%, daily-use apps 11.80%) — this is the safest middle ground for risk-averse teams, outperforming pure demos (3-5%) without the niche dependency of Hook+Demo (17-35%).
8. **Silent UGC + Niche Community Signaling** through hashtags drives 11-20% engagement (recovery app 9.86%, curiosity hashtag 18.14%) — strategic hashtag pairing is essential for reaching underserved audiences in lifestyle and health categories.
9. **Comparison Hooks** (app vs competitor positioning) achieve 11% engagement while building credibility (language app 7.60%, early access flex 11.14%) — position solutions and competitive advantages, not feature clarity.
10. **Rare Feature Reveals + Problem-Solving Positioning** drives 10-20% engagement (productivity 10.56%, app guides 11.56%) when highlighting solutions users actively seek — this outperforms aesthetic customization hooks by 3x.
11. **Text-Heavy Entertainment** generates viral reach (1M-14M views) but collapses to 1-3% engagement with zero conversion intent — avoid news hooks, political content, and viral trends entirely; they're conversion-toxic despite visibility.
12. **B2B and Finance apps underperform across all formats** (0.12-10.98% engagement) except when positioned as accessible/creative solutions (AI mastery 10.98%, side hustle 8.95%) — niche positioning and democratization messaging significantly outperform direct CTAs.
